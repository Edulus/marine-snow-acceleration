# Code of Conduct

This project is committed to respectful, evidence-based collaboration.

Participants are expected to:

- communicate respectfully,
- critique ideas rather than people,
- disclose uncertainty,
- avoid harassment,
- welcome interdisciplinary participation,
- treat ecological and governance concerns seriously.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, personal attacks, deliberate misinformation, and reckless promotion of unreviewed environmental deployment.

Maintainers may remove comments, issues, or contributions that violate these standards.
