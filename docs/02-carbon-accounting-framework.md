# Carbon Accounting Framework

## Purpose

This document defines how carbon claims are evaluated.

## Status

Draft placeholder.

## Governing Metric

> Net additional durable carbon stored at depth after ecological, chemical, operational, and lifecycle costs are counted.

## Required Distinctions

The project must distinguish between:

- carbon fixed,
- carbon exported,
- carbon stored,
- carbon stored durably,
- carbon removed additionally,
- carbon removed after uncertainty discounting.

## Basic Accounting Sequence

Gross photosynthetic carbon fixation  
minus carbon respired by organisms  
minus carbon remineralized in the upper ocean  
minus carbon recycled through grazing and microbial loops  
minus carbonate counter-pump effects  
minus lifecycle emissions  
equals net durable carbon removal.
