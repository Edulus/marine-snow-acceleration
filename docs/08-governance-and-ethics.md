# Governance and Ethics

## Purpose

This document defines governance and ethical constraints.

## Status

Draft placeholder.

## Commitments

Before open-ocean intervention, the project would require:

- public documentation,
- independent scientific review,
- environmental assessment,
- regulatory approval,
- community consultation where affected communities can be identified,
- transparent monitoring,
- third-party verification,
- shutdown authority,
- post-intervention reporting.

## Ethical Questions

- Who benefits?
- Who bears risk?
- Who consents?
- Who monitors?
- Who can stop the intervention?
- How are coastal benefits weighed against deep-ocean impacts?
- How is moral hazard avoided?
