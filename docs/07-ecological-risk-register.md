# Ecological Risk Register

## Purpose

This document identifies ecological risks and monitoring needs.

## Status

Draft placeholder.

## Initial Risk Categories

- Carbon underperformance
- Carbonate or chemical penalty
- Ecological disruption
- Persistence of engineered organisms
- Measurement failure
- Public opposition
- Transboundary harm
- Moral hazard
- Operational emissions
- Premature commercialization
- Ballast pollution
- Failed sargassum targeting
- Upper-ocean decomposition
- Deep-ocean oxygen stress
- Benthic smothering

## Risk Doctrine

Carbon is the target.

Ecological integrity is the boundary.

Under climate emergency conditions, localized ecological impacts may be considered only if they are measured, bounded, and clearly outweighed by verified climate and coastal-protection benefits.
